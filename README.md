## Project Overview
This repository serves as a comprehensive portfolio for the project. The goal of this initiative is to demonstrate and promote digital fluency, professional online communication, and cyber safety among peers. Through a series of practical tasks, this project outlines the transition from a passive technology consumer to a responsible, secure, and proactive digital citizen.

---

## Repository Structure & Module Summaries

### 1. [Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic](task-1-presentation.pdf)

**Summary:** A high-energy, visually engaging infographic designed using Canva. It breaks down the three pillars of digital literacy: defining critical evaluation, practising safe internet habits, and building a professional online presence.



### 2. [Task 2: Professional Online Presence](task-2-portfolio.pdf)

**Summary:** Establishing a foundational digital footprint for a career in software engineering. This module includes the creation and optimization of profiles on key industry platforms.
* **Platforms:** GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle.



### 3. [Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Tools](task-3-platforms.pdf)

**Summary:** A demonstration of practical coding skills and proficiency in cloud-based collaboration. Features a solved introductory Python challenge and a functional digital awareness quiz designed for peer assessment.
* **Tools Used:** HackerRank, Google Workspace (Forms & Sheets).
* **google form link:** https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe3TNThtq9ZZ-s6uC-ThhuNIAppvP711eHvnY4Uipajm_U9Jw/viewform?usp=publish-editor


### 4. [Task 4: Professional Communication](./task-4-email-etiquette)

**Summary:** An exploration of digital etiquette. This module contains drafted examples of professional email communication (academic and corporate) and a comprehensive "Do's and Don'ts" checklist for responsible social media usage.
* **Files:** `professional_emails.pdf`, `social-media-checklist.md`



### 5. [Task 5: Cybercrime & Online Safety](task-5-cybercrime)

**Summary:** An awareness module focusing on modern digital threats. It features a detailed case study on UPI "Receive Money" QR code scams targeting college students, alongside an actionable, 8-point prevention checklist.
* **Files:** `casestudy.md`, `prevention-checklist.md`
